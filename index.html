About Me

I'm Oliver Cole — a mature Computer Science (Games Development) student at the University of Plymouth and an aspiring game developer. After several years in finance I switched careers to follow my passion for games: I love building systems, solving design puzzles, and making playable experiences that teach or surprise players.

I draw most of my creative energy from cinematic, player-driven adventures — most notably the Star Wars Jedi games (Fallen Order, Survivor) and classic multiplayer favourites like Battlefront and Jedi Academy. I also enjoyed recent narrative-driven titles such as Indiana Jones: The Great Circle and look forward to upcoming releases like Tomb Raider and Star Wars: Fate of the Old Republic.

  • Current focus: Godot (GDScript) — building a 3D logic-gate puzzle for my COMP3000 final project
  • Other work: C++/SDL project (top-down shooter/bullet-hell)
  • C++/OpenGL project (first person exploration demo)

I enjoy rapid prototyping, iterative playtesting, and systems that reward player creativity.
